About Event

  • Did you go through job changes recently?
  • Are you concerned about job insecurity?
  • How are your financials and nesteggs doing during 2022?

This offline/online seminar walks you through a list of year-end planning items to better prepare you and your family for a more secure future, and position for a market rebound.

The seminar aims to answer the following questions:

  • Is now a good time to convert your 401K/IRA to Roth? (hint: SP500 is down 20% by 11/2022)
  • Did you take advantage of Backdoor Roth (applied to everyone) and Mega backdoor Roth (employer sponsored)?
  • What are the options for emergency funds?
  • In-transit healthcare: how to reduce your COBRA payment?
  • Thinking for the long term: options for cash flow, investment appreciation and alternative career path?
